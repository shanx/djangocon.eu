Open letter

To potential sponsors

Dear potential sponsor,

We would love to secure your sponsorship for DjangoCon Europe 2011: the annual European conference on the Django web framework, taking place this year in Amsterdam, the Netherlands.

Django is Open Source software, like Linux, that allows software engineers to rapidly develop websites quickly and easily. Using Django allows for smaller teams to get better work done in less time.

The conference

The conference will be held from Monday June 6th until Friday June 10th 2011. Three days will be used for keynotes, presentations and panel discussions at “Pakhuis de Zwijger”.

The final two days are organized at “De Waag”. These days will consist of development sprints, where the community comes together to improve Django itself. The sprint venue will be open non-stop for 48 hours; these marathon sessions are incredibly valuable as they bring the usually far-flung development community to one place for discussion and collaboration.

Sponsoring

DjangoCon attracts several hundred smart software engineers from across the globe, both in person and via streaming video. Support the conference and gain a place in their hearts and minds.

Contact

If you are interested in sponsoring DjangoCon Europe please don’t hesitate to contact us at sponsors@djangocon.eu.
