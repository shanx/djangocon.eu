DjangoCon Europe 2011

Conference

The European conference on the Django framework.

Welcome

This year the European Django community would like to welcome you all in the city of Amsterdam, the Netherlands.

Amsterdam has been known throughout history as a city of open mindedness, tolerance and trade. And we hope we can show this by organising a fabulous DjangoCon in the very heart of the city!

Focus: Advanced topics

Amsterdam will be the successor to two amazing editions held earlier: 2009 in Prague and2010 in Berlin.

Over the years the Django framework has shown a definate growth to becoming a mature framework with a wealthy ecosystem of plugins and applications developed by many talented developers, like you.

We want to honour this by organising a conference that focusses on advanced topics.
