{% endblock content %} The sprint days will be June 9-10 The sprint location is a place called De Waag which is an old castle in the very heart of Amsterdam, and is famous for housing the Amsterdam Guild of Surgeons during the 17th century. The sprint location will be open for us 48-hours non stop, meaning literally that we can sprint until we drop. If (when) you drop we have field beds so you can sleep on location. We will do everything we can to make sure the sprints will be as fun and productive as possible for all participants. This includes 1. Running up to the the conference we'll coordinate with the Core Developers to make sure we have stuff to do that's both useful and doable. 2. At least one of the talks on the main conference days will be dealing with contributing to Django in general and the sprint in particular 3. For those who are not familiar with sprints: we'll make sure to ease you into the experience on the actual sprint days. Sprints are going to be awesome!
