DjangoCon is sold out and the waiting list is closed.

Conference sold out, waiting list closed

As of this moment DjangoCon Europe 2011 is sold out and we have closed the waiting list. If you where not able to buy a ticket yet or put yourself on the waiting list, you'll have to follow DjangoCon comfortably from your chair using our live video stream.

You are on the waiting list, now what?

Starting May 18th we will start inviting people who have subscribed to the waiting list. People who put themselves on the waiting list first will be invited first to buy their ticket. From then on it is first-come, first-served.

Tickets are sold at the regular rate. The night owl rate has been scratched entirely.
